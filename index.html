About Us


Keeping It Spicy Since 1895

Welcome to Capsaicin Chinese, where culinary magic meets fiery flavors! Nestled in the heart of Chinatown, our restaurant is an ideal spot for spice enthusiasts and adventurous eaters alike. Our specialty at Capsaicin Chinese is creating traditional Chinese food with a spicy twist. Every dish is meticulously infused with an arrangement of spices that will stimulate your palate and leave you craving more. Every bite promises an amazing journey through the vibrant world of Chinese cuisine, from our aromatic Kung Pao chicken packed with explosive heat to our famous Szechuan Chicken with tongue-tingling spices. Come dine with us at Capsaicin Chinese, where flavor reigns supreme and every dish is irresistibly delicious!

From a family owned restaurant to a famous Chinese food chain! We first started as farmers growing our own vegetables and spices! With the compliments from people who tried our home cooking! We decided to create our very own restaurant using our secret recipes.
